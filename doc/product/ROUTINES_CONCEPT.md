# Routines -- Product Concept

> Audience: product + design + engineering
>
> Scope: product purpose and UX intent for the Routines screen.

## Primary purpose

"Routines is where you keep consistent practices that move your values forward."

## Description line (UI copy)

"Set up routines that keep your values moving forward, week by week."

## Key question it answers

"What routines support my values right now?"

## What it shows

"Routines shows your scheduled and flexible routines, with targets and
remaining counts."
